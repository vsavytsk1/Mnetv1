## THE ACTUAL DESIGN (stupid simple, Derek approved)

### CORE LOOP:
```
1. Open the app
2. See your C60 -- partially built, glowing in VR space
3. See which faces are open (the unsolved concepts)
4. Pick an open pentagon (a hard concept)
5. Enter the math tree for that concept
6. Walk the branches -- click, explore, dead ends teach
7. Solve the tree -- face closes on the C60
8. See the shell get closer to complete
9. When all 12 pentagons closed -- chi=2 -- SHELL CLOSES
10. The shape snaps together. Euler wins. You win.
```

### WIN CONDITION:
  V - E + F = 2
  P = 12
  ALL 12 pentagons closed = game complete
  This is not arbitrary. Euler FORCES this to be the ending.
  The math chooses when the game ends.

### DIFFICULTY:
  Pentagon (hard concept) = costs more tokens to unlock branches
  Hexagon (easier path)   = cheaper, but doesn't close the face
  You MUST go through a pentagon to close its face
  Dead ends cost nothing -- they teach for free

### THE VALTIUM TWIST:
  Load your Obsidian vault at the start
  Your own notes become the hexagons
  Your own research fills the faces
  The game is literally your knowledge graph
  chi=2 of YOUR MIND closes the shell
