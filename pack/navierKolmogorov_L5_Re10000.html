THE PHYSICS

• Vorticity equation: dω/dt + J(ψ,ω) = ν∇²ω + f
• Poisson solve: ∇²ψ = -ω (CG iterative, 50 max iter)
• Jacobian advection: J ≈ A@(ψ)·ω - ψ·A@(ω) (Arakawa-like on graph)
• Graph Laplacian: L = A_norm - I (sparse, trivalent)
• Forcing: Gaussian noise on 5% of faces per step
• Time integration: Forward Euler, dt=0.005
• Kolmogorov cascade: Energy injected at large scale, dissipated at small scale by ν

WHAT THIS IS

• A 2D vorticity-streamfunction solver on a closed spherical graph
• Kolmogorov forced turbulence with energy injection + viscous dissipation
• Nonlinear: the Jacobian J(ψ,ω) couples vorticity to velocity
• The energy spectrum should approach k-5/3 at statistical equilibrium

WHAT THIS IS NOT

• Not 3D turbulence (2D vorticity on a surface)
• Not a DNS code (graph discretization, not finite volume/element)
• Not a proof of Kolmogorov theory (it is a numerical observation)
• Not a claim of discovery (all physics attributed to Kolmogorov 1941)
